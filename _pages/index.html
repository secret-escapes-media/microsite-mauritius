Sublime Landscapes

Norway offers landscapes that will leave you reeling – crystalline fjords mark the base of towering peaks, dramatic in their splendour and rugged disposition. Vast sheets of tumbling ice form epic glaciers that deserve exploring, whilst a patchwork of islands can be hopped about on offering seclusion in the serene landscape of Norway.

Discover Sunnmøre

Leading Art & Architecture

World-leading galleries, museums and artists permeate the cultural landscape of Norway, which frames them in unique and compelling architecture. From the ultra-modern Barcode and Oslo Opera house, to the traditional, technicolour Bryggen buildings and the historic Stave churches dotted around the country, Norway is awash with resplendent design.

Discover Oslo

Vibrant History

Norway’s landscape has dictated its seafaring history. From the quaint fishing villages that time seemingly forgot, to the rich Viking heritage that provides a proud backdrop to the traditions and culture of its people, Norway has a prolific heritage that invites discovery.
